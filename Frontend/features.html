Features : What we Provide

False Urgency

“False Urgency” refers to the deceptive practice of falsely conveying or implying a sense of urgency or scarcity to mislead users into making immediate purchases or taking prompt actions, potentially resulting in a purchase.


Solution : Dark Transparency fixes this problem by using AI to provide you with the actual original results of the product.

Basket Sneaking

Basket Sneaking has been defined as the practice of including additional items, such as products, donations, during the checkout process on a platform without the explicit consent of the user. This results in the total amount payable by the user exceeding the amount intended for the chosen the product or service.


Solution : Our extension fixes this problem by highlighting the additional items so as to make it visible to the user that additional money is being charged to them.

Confirm Shaming

Confirm shaming is the practice of employing phrases, videos, audio, or any other means to instill a sense of fear, shame, ridicule, or guilt in the user’s mind. The objective is achieved through emotional manipulation by subtly pushing the user to perform a specific action which they would not have done otherwise.


Solution : Our extension fixes this problem by highlighting the second option as well from both the choices.

Forced Action

Forced Action refers to the practice of compelling a user to take an action that necessitates purchasing additional goods, subscribing to unrelated services, signing personal information.


Solution : Our extension generates a prompt if the user checks a checkbox that might put their personal information at risk.

Subscription Trap

A “Subscription Trap” involves tactics such as masking the cancellation of a paid subscription overly complex or impossible, hiding the cancellation option, compelling users to provide payment details for auto-debits even ostensibly free subscriptions, and employing ambiguous or confusing instructions regarding the cancellation process..


Solution : Our extension solves this problem by sending an email to the user 2-3 days before their free trial is about to expire.

Bait and Switch

Bait and switch is a deceptive practice that involves advertising a specific outcome based on the user’s action and then providing an alternative outcome that differs from what was initially presented.


Solution : Our extension solves this problem by generating a pop up which warns the user that their information might get shared by clicking on that particular option.

Trick Questions

Trick Question refers to the intentional use of confusing or vague language, such as confusing wording, double negatives, or other similar tricks, with an aim to mislead or divert a user from taking the desired action or prompting a specific response or action.


Solution : Our extension gives warning to the user if extra purchases have been added with the recurring bill. It warns the user if extra purchases have been added on to their final bill.

Rogue Malware

Rogue Malwares involve the use of ransomware or scareware to deceive users by making them believe their computer is infected with a virus. The ultimate goal is to persuade them to pay for a fake malware removal tool, which, in reality, installs additional malware on their computer.


Solution : Our extension removes fake malware pop ups and highlights the close button on the popup which aids the user to close the popup with accuracy
